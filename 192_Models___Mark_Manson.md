# Models: Attract Women Through Honesty by Mark Manson

## 1. Long Detailed Summary for Blog

**Title:** A Guide to Dating Based on Vulnerability and Integrity: *Models*

**Introduction**
Mark Manson challenges the "Pick-Up Artist" (PUA) industry. He argues that attraction is not about tricks or lines; it's about being an attractive person. True attraction is based on **Vulnerability** and **Non-Neediness**.

**The Core Philosophy**
1.  **Vulnerability is Power:** Most men try to look "perfect" or "alpha." Manson says the most attractive thing a man can do is be honest about his feelings and his flaws. It shows he is comfortable in his own skin.
2.  **Non-Neediness:** An attractive man is less invested in other people’s opinions of him than in his own opinion of himself. He doesn't "need" the girl's approval to be happy.
3.  **Demographics and Lifestyle:** You should live a life that naturally brings you into contact with people you like. Work on your fitness, your career, and your fashion for *yourself*, not for others.
4.  **Polarization:** Don't try to please everyone. Be bold enough to show who you are so that the people who aren't a match leave quickly, and the ones who are stay.

**Conclusion**
Dating is a process of self-improvement. By becoming a better man, you naturally attract better women.
