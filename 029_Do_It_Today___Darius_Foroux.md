# Do It Today by Darius Foroux

## 1. Long Detailed Summary for Blog

**Title:** Overcoming Procrastination and Improving Productivity: *Do It Today*

**Introduction**
Darius Foroux’s *Do It Today* is a compilation of articles on procrastination. It’s practical, direct, and focused on the psychology of delaying work.

**Key Insights**
1.  **Procrastination is an Emotion:** We don't procrastinate because we are lazy; we do it to avoid negative emotions (fear of failure, boredom).
2.  **Time Blocking:** Standard advice, but crucial.
3.  **The Price of 'Tomorrow':** Tomorrow is a fantasy land where you are energetic and disciplined. Today is reality.
4.  **Disconnect:** You cannot do deep work while connected to the hive mind.

**Strategies**
*   **Evening Ritual:** Plan tomorrow tonight.
*   **Exercise:** It’s the foundation of mental energy.
*   **Consistency:** Small daily actions beat intensity.

**Conclusion**
There is no secret. Just do it today.

**Script 1: The Trap of Tomorrow**
*   **Audio:** "Tomorrow is a trap. It’s a magical land where you are perfect. But when tomorrow comes, it’s just 'Today' again, and you are still tired. Stop trusting your future self. He is unreliable. Do the hard thing now. Today is the only time you have."

**Script 2: Procrastination is Fear**
*   **Audio:** "You aren't lazy. You are scared. Procrastination is an emotional reaction. You are avoiding the stress of the task. The only way out is through. Acknowledge the fear, and take one tiny step. Action cures fear."

**Script 3: Evening Routine**
*   **Audio:** "A good morning starts the night before. If you wake up and have to decide what to do, you’ve already lost. Spend 10 minutes every night writing down your 3 priorities for tomorrow. Wake up with a mission, not a question."

**Script 4: Consistency**
*   **Audio:** "You don't need to be amazing. You just need to be consistent. Writing 1 page a day is a book in a year. Writing 20 pages once and quitting is nothing. Embrace the boring power of consistency."

**Script 5: Disconnect**
