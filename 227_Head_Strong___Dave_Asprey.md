# Head Strong by Dave Asprey

## 1. Long Detailed Summary for Blog

**Title:** The Bulletproof Plan to Activate Untapped Brain Energy: *Head Strong*

**Introduction**
Dave Asprey, creator of Bulletproof Coffee, focuses on "Neurogenesis"—the growth of new brain cells. He argues that brain fog, forgetfulness, and fatigue are not signs of aging; they are signs of "Mitochondrial Dysfunction."

**Key Insights**
1.  **The Brain is an Energy Hog:** The brain uses 20% of your total energy. If your mitochondria are weak, your brain is the first thing to suffer.
2.  **Inflammation is the Enemy:** Toxins in food (like mold in coffee or sugar) cause brain inflammation, which slows down your thinking.
3.  **Light as Food:** The brain needs specific frequencies of light (infrared) and needs to avoid others (blue light at night).
4.  **Ketosis:** The brain runs more efficiently on ketones (fats) than on glucose (sugar).
5.  **Polyphenols:** Dark chocolate, coffee, and colorful vegetables are "brain food."

**Conclusion**
You can hack your biology to increase your IQ and your energy. It all starts with the mitochondria.
