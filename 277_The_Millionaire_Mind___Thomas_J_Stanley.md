# The Millionaire Mind by Thomas J. Stanley

## 1. Long Detailed Summary for Blog

**Title:** How to Think Like a Multi-Millionaire: *The Millionaire Mind*

**Introduction**
In his follow-up to *The Millionaire Next Door*, Dr. Stanley looks at the "Top 1%"—those with a net worth over \$10 million. He discovers that their mindset is fundamentally different from the middle class. Success at this level is about social skills, discipline, and choosing the right spouse.

**Key Insights**
1.  **Integrity and Discipline:** These were rated higher than "Intelligence" as factors for success. The wealthy are obsessive about being honest and being disciplined.
2.  **Social Skills:** Multi-millionaires are "people persons." They know how to negotiate, how to lead, and how to deal with critics.
3.  **The Spouse:** Choosing the right life partner is the most important financial decision you will ever make. Most multi-millionaires have been married to the same person for 20+ years.
4.  **Academic Underperformers:** Most of these people were not straight-A students. They were told they weren't smart as kids, which forced them to develop "Grit" and better social skills to survive.
5.  **Courage:** The willingness to take risks and handle fear.

**Conclusion**
The millionaire mind is built on character and relationships, not just spreadsheets.
