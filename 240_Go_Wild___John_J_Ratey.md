# Go Wild by John J. Ratey

## 1. Long Detailed Summary for Blog

**Title:** Free Your Body and Mind from the Afflictions of Civilization: *Go Wild*

**Introduction**
John Ratey and Richard Manning argue that most of our modern illnesses—obesity, diabetes, anxiety, and insomnia—are "Diseases of Civilization." Our DNA is that of hunter-gatherers, but we live in a world of cubes, screens, and processed sugar. To thrive, we must "re-wild" our lives.

**Key Insights**
1.  **The Wild Diet:** Eat real, whole foods. Avoid the "Agricultural Trap" of wheat, corn, and soy. Your body wants wild plants and wild-caught protein.
2.  **Move like an Animal:** Stop the repetitive motions of the gym. Climb, crawl, carry, and jump. Move in nature.
3.  **The Sleep of the Jungle:** We are light-polluted. We need total darkness and to follow the sun's rhythm to heal our hormones.
4.  **Connect with the Tribe:** Humans are not meant to be alone. Social isolation is a biological stressor. We need community to survive.
5.  **Biophilia:** We have an innate love for nature. Being near trees and water reduces stress markers instantly.

**Conclusion**
We are wild animals living in a zoo. The doors are open. It’s time to step outside.

If you are feeling burned out, go to the nearest park and just sit. It’s medicine."
