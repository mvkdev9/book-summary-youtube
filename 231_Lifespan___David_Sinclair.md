# Lifespan by David Sinclair

## 1. Long Detailed Summary for Blog

**Title:** Why We Age—and Why We Don't Have To: *Lifespan*

**Introduction**
Dr. David Sinclair, a leading geneticist at Harvard, proposes a "Unified Theory of Aging." He argues that aging is a disease, caused by the loss of information in our cells (The Information Theory of Aging). Most importantly, he claims that aging is treatable and reversible.

**Key Insights**
1.  **The Information Theory of Aging:** Cells don't lose their DNA; they lose the ability to read it correctly. It’s like a scratched CD. The "Epigenome" (the reader) gets distracted by repairing damage and forgets its original function.
2.  **Sirtuins:** A family of proteins that act as the "guardians of the genome." They require **NAD+** to function. As we age, NAD+ levels drop, and Sirtuins stop working.
3.  **Hormesis:** "What doesn't kill you makes you live longer." Stressing the body (in small doses) activates survival genes.
4.  **The Anti-Aging Toolkit:**
    *   *Intermittent Fasting:* Triggers autophagy and Sirtuins.
    *   *Exercise:* High-intensity interval training (HIIT) is best.
    *   *Cold/Heat Exposure:* Sauna and cold plunges.
    *   *Molecules:* NMN, Resveratrol, and Metformin (under medical supervision).

**Conclusion**
Aging is not a law of nature. By activating our survival circuits, we can extend our healthy years significantly.
