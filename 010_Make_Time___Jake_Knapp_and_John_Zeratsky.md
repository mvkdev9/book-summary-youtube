# Make Time by Jake Knapp and John Zeratsky

## 1. Long Detailed Summary for Blog

**Title:** How to Focus on What Matters Every Day: *Make Time*

**Introduction**
From the creators of the Google Venture "Design Sprint," *Make Time* isn't about productivity (doing more). It’s about doing what matters. The authors argue that we are beset by two forces: The Busy Bandwagon (the culture of constant busyness) and The Infinity Pools (apps with endless content like Instagram/Email).

**The 4-Step Framework**
1.  **Highlight:** Start every day by choosing a single focal point. Not the only thing you'll do, but the one thing that will make the day a success.
    *   *Types of Highlights:* Urgency (has to be done), Satisfaction (want to get done), Joy (just for fun).
2.  **Laser:** Beat distraction to make time for your Highlight.
    *   *Tactics:* Delete social apps from your phone (The Distraction-Free Phone), Log out of everything, Turn off notifications. creating barriers to the Infinity Pools.
3.  **Energize:** You need energy to focus.
    *   *Tactics:* Move your body (even walking helps), Eat real food, Caffeine timing (don't crash), Disconnect (go offline).
4.  **Reflect:** Adjust and improve. At the end of the day, take notes. Did you do your Highlight? How was your energy? What will you change tomorrow?

**Key Concepts**
*   **The Busy Bandwagon:** The default mode of modern life where we fill every minute with "productivity" but achieve little meaning.
*   **Infinity Pools:** Any app or service that has an endless supply of content (Netflix, Twitter, Email). They will eat all your time if you let them.
*   **Distraction-Free Phone:** A phone with no browser, no email, and no social media. Only tools (Maps, Uber, Music).

**Conclusion**
You don't need more time. You need to make time for what matters. It’s a daily practice of intention.

## 3. Short Form Video Scripts (30-60 Seconds)

**Script 1: The Daily Highlight**
*   **Audio:** "Toss your to-do list. It’s stressing you out. Instead, pick a 'Daily Highlight.' Ask yourself: 'What is the one thing I want to remember about today?' Maybe it’s finishing that presentation, or maybe it’s taking your dog to the park. If you get that one thing done, the day is a success. Focus on the Highlight, and let the rest happen."

**Script 2: Infinity Pools**
*   **Audio:** "Your phone is an 'Infinity Pool.' You can scroll Instagram, TikTok, or Email forever, and it will never end. That’s why you lose hours of your life. The solution? Create barriers. Delete the apps. Log out. Make it annoying to access them. If you have to log in every time, you won’t do it. Drain the pool."

**Script 3: The Distraction-Free Phone**
*   **Audio:** "Want to change your life in 5 minutes? Delete all social media and email from your phone. Seriously. Turn your phone into a tool, not a trap. Keep Maps, Music, and Uber. Delete everything else. If you need to check Instagram, do it on your computer. Reclaim your attention. Be the boss of your phone."

**Script 4: Energize**
*   **Audio:** "You don't need better time management; you need better energy management. If you are tired, 5 hours of free time is useless. *Make Time* suggests simple hacks: Walk everyday. Eat real food. And stop drinking coffee *after* you get tired—drink it 30 minutes before the crash. Treat your body like a battery, not a machine."

**Script 5: The Busy Bandwagon**
*   **Audio:** "Society pushes us onto the 'Busy Bandwagon.' We value being busy over being effective. We answer emails instantly to look productive. But looking productive is not the same as making a difference. Jump off the bandwagon. Be slow. Be intentional. Do one thing that matters, rather than 10 things that don't."
