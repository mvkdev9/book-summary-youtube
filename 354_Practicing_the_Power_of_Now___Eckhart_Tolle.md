# Practicing the Power of Now by Eckhart Tolle

## 1. Long Detailed Summary for Blog

**Title:** The Manual for Enlightenment: *Practicing the Power of Now*

**Introduction**
While *The Power of Now* explains the philosophy of enlightenment, *Practicing the Power of Now* is the hands-on manual. It extracts the exercises and meditations from the original book to give readers a practical toolkit for accessing the present moment.

**Core Concepts**

1.  **Accessing the Now:**
    *   Tolle gives specific techniques: Feel the energy in your hands and feet (Inner Body awareness). Listen to silence. Accept whatever arises.
    *   These anchors pull you out of the mind and into the body/present.

2.  **Dissolving the Pain-Body:**
    *   The "Pain-Body" is the accumulation of old emotional pain living in your energy field. It feeds on negative thinking.
    *   To dissolve it, you must "shine the light of consciousness" on it. Notice it when it wakes up (usually when you are irritated). Don't judge it; just watch it. It cannot survive presence.

3.  **Surrender:**
    *   Surrender is not weakness; it is strength. It means accepting the "is-ness" of the moment.
    *   If you are stuck in mud, surrender doesn't mean staying there; it means acknowledging "I am in mud" without panic, so you can calmly get out.

4.  **Relationships as Spiritual Practice:**
    *   Stop looking for "fulfillment" in a partner. Use the relationship to become conscious. When your partner triggers you, use it as an opportunity to watch your own ego reaction.

**Conclusion**
This book is for those who ask, "Okay, I get the concept, but *how* do I do it?" It turns the philosophy into a daily habit.
