# Breath by James Nestor

## 1. Long Detailed Summary for Blog

**Title:** The New Science of a Lost Art: *Breath*

**Introduction**
James Nestor argues that we have lost the ability to breathe correctly. 90% of humans breathe through their mouths, which leads to sleep apnea, high blood pressure, and ADHD. By re-learning how to breathe through our noses, we can transform our health and even our face structure.

**Key Insights**
1.  **Mouth Breathing is Toxic:** It dries out the lungs, lowers oxygen absorption, and changes the shape of your jaw.
2.  **The Nose is a Filter:** The nose warms, humidifies, and filters the air. It also produces Nitric Oxide, which boosts circulation.
3.  **Breathe Less:** Over-breathing (too many breaths per minute) causes stress. The optimal rate is about 5.5 breaths per minute.
4.  **Carbon Dioxide is Good:** We think CO2 is a waste product, but it is necessary for oxygen to enter our cells (The Bohr Effect). Holding your breath can improve your tolerance and endurance.
5.  **Chewing:** Our ancestors had perfect teeth because they chewed hard food. Modern "soft" diets lead to narrow jaws and crooked teeth.

**Conclusion**
The most important pillar of health is the one we do 25,000 times a day. Breathe through your nose, slowly.
