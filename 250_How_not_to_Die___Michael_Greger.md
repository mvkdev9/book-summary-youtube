# How Not to Die by Michael Greger

## 1. Long Detailed Summary for Blog

**Title:** Discover the Foods Scientifically Proven to Prevent and Reverse Disease: *How Not to Die*

**Introduction**
Dr. Michael Greger analyzes the top 15 causes of death in the US (heart disease, cancer, diabetes, etc.) and provides the scientific evidence that a **Whole-Food, Plant-Based (WFPB) diet** can prevent and, in many cases, reverse them.

**Key Insights**
1.  **Diet trumps Genes:** You might have the "bad genes" for heart disease, but your diet determines if those genes are expressed. "Genes load the gun, but lifestyle pulls the trigger."
2.  **Heart Disease is Reversible:** A plant-based diet can literally unclog your arteries without surgery or drugs.
3.  **The Daily Dozen:** Greger’s checklist of what to eat every day:
    *   Beans (3 servings)
    *   Berries (1 serving)
    *   Other Fruits (3 servings)
    *   Cruciferous Veggies (1 serving)
    *   Greens (2 servings)
    *   Other Veggies (2 servings)
    *   Flaxseeds (1 tbsp)
    *   Nuts (1 serving)
    *   Spices (Turmeric!)
    *   Whole Grains (3 servings)
    *   Beverages (Water/Tea)
    *   Exercise (90 mins light or 40 mins intense)

**Conclusion**
The most powerful medicine is found at the end of your fork.
