# The Diabetes Code by Jason Fung

## 1. Long Detailed Summary for Blog

**Title:** Prevent and Reverse Type 2 Diabetes Naturally: *The Diabetes Code*

**Introduction**
Dr. Jason Fung argues that Type 2 Diabetes is a reversible disease. Most doctors treat it as chronic and progressive, but Fung shows that it is a disease of "Too Much Sugar." By changing our lifestyle and using intermittent fasting, we can eliminate the need for insulin and medication.

**Key Insights**
1.  **The Overflow Phenomenon:** Type 2 Diabetes is not a "lack" of insulin; it is a "surplus" of sugar. Your cells are already full of glucose, and your body is trying to shove more in.
2.  **The Big Lie:** Giving insulin to a Type 2 diabetic is like putting more luggage into an already overflowing closet. It lowers blood sugar by forcing the sugar into your tissues, where it causes damage (blindness, kidney failure).
3.  **Fructose is the Enemy:** Unlike glucose, fructose is processed 100% in the liver, leading to "Fatty Liver," which is the first step toward diabetes.
4.  **The Cure:** Stop putting sugar in! Use low-carb diets and therapeutic fasting to let the body "burn off" the excess sugar already stored in the system.

**Conclusion**
Type 2 Diabetes is a dietary disease. It requires a dietary solution, not a pharmaceutical one.
