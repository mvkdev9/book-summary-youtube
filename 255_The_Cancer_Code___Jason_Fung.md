# The Cancer Code by Jason Fung

## 1. Long Detailed Summary for Blog

**Title:** A Revolutionary New Understanding of a Medical Mystery: *The Cancer Code*

**Introduction**
Dr. Jason Fung explores the history of cancer research and identifies three "paradigms":
1.  **Paradigm 1: Cancer as a Growth.** (Treat with surgery/radiation).
2.  **Paradigm 2: Cancer as a Genetic Mutation.** (Treat with chemotherapy). This paradigm mostly failed because cancer mutates too fast.
3.  **Paradigm 3: Cancer as an Evolutionary Seed.** Cancer isn't a "broken" cell; it's a cell that has reverted to an ancient, primitive survival mode. It thrives in a specific "Soil."

**Key Insights**
*   **The Soil:** Cancer needs a specific environment to grow. That environment is high in **Insulin** and **Inflammation**.
*   **Insulin as a Growth Factor:** Hyperinsulinemia (too much insulin) is a primary driver of cancer. Obesity is linked to 13 types of cancer because of high insulin levels.
*   **Metabolic Syndrome:** Fixing your metabolism is the best way to prevent cancer.
*   **Prevention:** Fasting and low-carb diets reduce the "Growth Signals" that cancer cells need to multiply.

**Conclusion**
We may never "cure" cancer with a single drug, but we can change the "Soil" of our bodies so that cancer cannot thrive.
