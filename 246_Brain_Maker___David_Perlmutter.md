# Brain Maker by David Perlmutter

## 1. Long Detailed Summary for Blog

**Title:** The Power of Gut Microbes to Heal and Protect Your Brain—for Life: *Brain Maker*

**Introduction**
Following *Grain Brain*, Dr. Perlmutter explores the "Gut-Brain Connection." He argues that the health of your brain is determined by the health of the 100 trillion microbes living in your gut (the Microbiome).

**Key Insights**
1.  **The Gut-Brain Axis:** The gut and brain are connected via the Vagus nerve and the immune system. 90% of your serotonin is produced in your gut.
2.  **Dysbiosis:** An imbalance of gut bacteria. This leads to brain inflammation, which causes autism, ADHD, Alzheimer's, and depression.
3.  **The Killers:** Antibiotics, sugar, and environmental toxins (like glyphosate) destroy your "Good" bacteria.
4.  **The Brain Makers:** Probiotics and fermented foods.
5.  **Fecal Microbiota Transplant (FMT):** A radical but effective treatment for severe neurological issues by transplanting healthy gut bacteria.

**The Solution (The 5 Pillars)**
*   Lower your carbs.
*   Eat high-quality fats.
*   Eat prebiotic fiber (to feed the good bacteria).
*   Eat fermented foods (Kimchi, Sauerkraut).
*   Filter your water.

**Conclusion**
The path to a healthy brain goes through the stomach.
