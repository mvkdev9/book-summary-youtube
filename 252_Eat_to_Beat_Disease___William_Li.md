# Eat to Beat Disease by William Li

## 1. Long Detailed Summary for Blog

**Title:** The New Science of How Your Body Can Heal Itself: *Eat to Beat Disease*

**Introduction**
Dr. William Li, a world-renowned physician and researcher, identifies five defense systems in our body that keep us healthy: **Angiogenesis**, **Regeneration**, **Microbiome**, **DNA Protection**, and **Immunity**. He argues that we can "activate" these systems through the specific foods we eat.

**The Five Defense Systems**
1.  **Angiogenesis:** The process of growing new blood vessels. Some foods (like berries and green tea) prevent tumors from growing their own blood supply.
2.  **Regeneration:** We have 750 million stem cells that repair our organs. Foods like dark chocolate and olive oil stimulate these cells.
3.  **Microbiome:** The trillions of bacteria in our gut. Fiber and fermented foods keep them healthy.
4.  **DNA Protection:** Our DNA is under constant attack. Antioxidants in vegetables protect and repair our genetic code.
5.  **Immunity:** Our internal army. Mushrooms, garlic, and broccoli sprout boost our ability to fight infection and cancer.

**The 5x5x5 Framework**
Eat 5 foods from his "Power List" every day, targeting all 5 defense systems.

**Conclusion**
Food is medicine. By choosing the right ingredients, we can prevent and even reverse chronic disease.
