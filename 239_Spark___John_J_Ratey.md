# Spark: The Revolutionary New Science of Exercise and the Brain by John J. Ratey

## 1. Long Detailed Summary for Blog

**Title:** How Exercise Improves Your IQ and Mental Health: *Spark*

**Introduction**
Dr. John Ratey argues that the primary purpose of exercise is not to build a body, but to build a brain. Exercise provides a "miracle-gro" for your neurons, making you smarter, happier, and more focused.

**Key Insights**
1.  **BDNF (Brain-Derived Neurotrophic Factor):** Ratey calls this "Miracle-Gro" for the brain. Exercise triggers the release of BDNF, which repairs brain cells and allows new ones to grow.
2.  **Learning:** You should exercise *before* you study. The brain is most primed for new information right after a workout.
3.  **Anxiety and Depression:** Exercise is as effective as Zoloft or Prozac for mild-to-moderate depression. It regulates neurotransmitters like Serotonin, Dopamine, and Norepinephrine.
4.  **ADHD:** Exercise provides the structure and dopamine that ADHD brains crave.
5.  **Aging:** Regular exercise can delay or prevent the onset of Alzheimer's and dementia.

**Conclusion**
Exercise is the most powerful psychiatric drug we have. Move your body to fix your mind.
