# Your Word is Your Wand by Florence Scovel Shinn

## 1. Long Detailed Summary for Blog

**Title:** Magic in Your Mouth: *Your Word is Your Wand*

**Introduction**
This is the sequel to *The Game of Life*. It is essentially a book of affirmations and "treatments" for specific situations. Shinn emphasizes that the subconscious mind is a faithful servant—it creates exactly what you tell it to create, without judgment.

**Core Concepts**

1.  **The Power of the Spoken Word:**
    *   "By your words you are justified, and by your words you are condemned."
    *   Talking about your troubles multiplies them. Talking about your blessings multiplies them.

2.  **Affirmations for Needs:**
    *   **Health:** "I am a spiritual being, my body is perfect, made in his image and likeness."
    *   **Wealth:** "I have a magical work in a magical way, I give magical service for magical pay."
    *   **Love:** "I draw to myself my own. My true love is now coming to me."

3.  **Intuition:**
    *   Intuition is the "tuition from within." It is God guiding you. You must learn to distinguish the hunch from the fear.

**Conclusion**
*Your Word is Your Wand* is a pocketbook of magic. It reminds us that we are always commanding our ship with our words, so we better learn to give the right orders.
