# The 4-Hour Body by Timothy Ferriss

## 1. Long Detailed Summary for Blog

**Title:** An Uncommon Guide to Rapid Fat-Loss, Incredible Sex, and Becoming Superhuman: *The 4-Hour Body*

**Introduction**
Tim Ferriss applies the 80/20 principle to the human body. He spent 10 years and \$250,000 on experiments to find the "Minimum Effective Dose" (MED) for everything from losing 20 lbs of fat to adding 30 lbs of muscle.

**Key Protocols**
1.  **The Slow-Carb Diet:** Eat the same 5-6 meals over and over. Avoid white carbs, fruit, and dairy. Drink lots of water. Have one "Cheat Day" a week. This is the core of his weight loss plan.
2.  **The 15-Minute Workout:** Ferriss argues that you don't need hours in the gym. He suggests 2 kettlebell swings and 2 sets of deadlifts per week to trigger "The Harajuku Effect" (massive change).
3.  **Occam's Protocol:** For adding muscle. Focus on very slow movements and high protein intake.
4.  **Biological Hacking:** Cold exposure to burn fat (Ice baths), PAGG stacks (supplements), and measuring everything (dexa scans, blood work).
5.  **Sleep:** How to achieve "polyphasic" sleep or just better deep sleep.

**Conclusion**
You don't have to be a professional athlete to have a world-class body. You just need to follow the right data.
