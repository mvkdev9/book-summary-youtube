# The Third Side by William Ury

## 1. Long Detailed Summary for Blog

**Title:** Why We Fight and How We Can Stop: *The Third Side*

**Introduction**
In his most ambitious work, William Ury argues that conflict isn't just between two parties (Side A and Side B). There is always a **Third Side**—the community around the conflict. The Third Side has the power to prevent, resolve, and contain any battle.

**The Roles of the Third Side**
1.  **Preventers:** (The Bridge-builder, The Teacher). They fix the roots of the problem before it starts.
2.  **Resolvers:** (The Mediator, The Arbiter). They help people talk and find a solution.
3.  **Containors:** (The Witness, The Peacekeeper). They stop the violence if it has already started.

**Key Insight: Conflict is Voluntary**
We think war and fighting are human nature. Ury shows that for 99% of human history, we were hunter-gatherers who *avoided* conflict because we needed each other to survive. Conflict only became chronic with the rise of property and hierarchy. We can go back to that cooperative state.

**Conclusion**
We all have a role to play in the peace of our families, our companies, and our world. Don't just pick a side; be the Third Side.
