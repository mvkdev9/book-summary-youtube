# Grain Brain by David Perlmutter

## 1. Long Detailed Summary for Blog

**Title:** The Surprising Truth about Wheat, Carbs, and Sugar—Your Brain's Silent Killers: *Grain Brain*

**Introduction**
Neurologist David Perlmutter argues that the standard high-carb diet is destroying our brains. He claims that gluten, sugar, and carbs are the root causes of Alzheimer’s, depression, and ADHD. The brain doesn't want "whole grains"; it wants **Fat**.

**Key Insights**
1.  **Carbs = Inflammation:** When you eat carbs, your blood sugar spikes. This triggers insulin, which causes chronic inflammation. Brain cells are highly sensitive to this inflammation.
2.  **Alzheimer’s is "Type 3 Diabetes":** Perlmutter argues that Alzheimer's is the result of long-term insulin resistance in the brain.
3.  **Gluten is a Toxin:** Not just for people with Celiac disease. Gluten causes "leaky gut" and "leaky brain" in almost everyone, leading to cognitive decline.
4.  **Cholesterol is Good:** We've been lied to. Your brain is made of cholesterol. Low cholesterol is linked to depression and memory loss.
5.  **The Solution:** A high-fat, low-carb, gluten-free diet. Eat avocados, eggs, and grass-fed meat.

**Conclusion**
You can prevent brain disease through your fork. Stop the grains to save your brain.
