# Super Human by Dave Asprey

## 1. Long Detailed Summary for Blog

**Title:** The Bulletproof Plan to Age Backward and Maybe Even Live Forever: *Super Human*

**Introduction**
Dave Asprey argues that death is just another technical problem to be solved. He identifies the "Seven Pillars of Aging" (e.g., Glycation, Mitochondrial loss, Stem cell exhaustion) and provides a blueprint to fix them. He claims that living to 180 is not only possible but likely.

**Key Insights**
1.  **Stop "Dying" Today:** You can't live forever if you are actively killing yourself. Stop eating fried food, stop drinking tap water, and stop being in the dark.
2.  **Mitochondria (Again):** Your longevity is tied to your energy production. If your cells are strong, you stay young.
3.  **Stem Cells:** Asprey discusses the power of banking your own stem cells and using them to repair injuries and internal organs.
4.  **Hormones:** As we age, our hormones drop. Asprey suggests HRT (Hormone Replacement Therapy) to keep your levels at "optimal" rather than "normal."
5.  **Light and Air:** He discusses the dangers of indoor air pollution and the benefits of hyperbaric oxygen therapy.

**Conclusion**
Aging is a disease. By treating the pillars of aging, you can stay biologically young while the calendar keeps moving.
