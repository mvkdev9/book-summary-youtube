# Peak Performance by Brad Stulberg and Steve Magness

## 1. Long Detailed Summary for Blog

**Title:** The Science of Success: *Peak Performance*

**Introduction**
Brad Stulberg (health consultant) and Steve Magness (performance coach) combine science and practice to find the formula for sustainable success.

**The Equation**
**Stress + Rest = Growth.**
*   This applies to muscles (lifting weights) and skills (learning math).
*   Most people either stress too much (burnout) or rest too much (complacency). You need the cycle.

**Key Insights**
1.  **Priming:** Set up a routine before your work to tell your brain "It's time."
2.  **Minimalist Lifestyle:** Decision fatigue kills performance. Simplify your life (Steve Jobs' turtleneck) to save energy for the work.
3.  **Purpose:** Ego-driven motivation burns out. Purpose-driven motivation (doing it for others) lasts.

**Strategies**
*   **Periodization:** Alternate between hard days and easy days.
*   **Sleep:** The best performance enhancer.
*   **Transcending Self:** Connect your work to a cause greater than yourself.

**Conclusion**
Consistency beats intensity. Manage the cycle of stress and rest.

*   **Host:** "Don't just grind. Pulse. Stress. Rest. Grow."

## 3. Short Form Video Scripts (30-60 Seconds)

**Script 1: Stress + Rest = Growth**
*   **Audio:** "Here is the universal formula for growth: Stress + Rest = Growth. It works for muscles. It works for learning. It works for business. You need the Stress (challenge) to break you down, and the Rest (recovery) to build you back stronger. If you skip the Stress, you stagnate. If you skip the Rest, you burn out. Balance the equation."

**Script 2: Just Manageable Challenges**
*   **Audio:** "How do you get better? You need 'Just Manageable Challenges.' Not too easy (boring). Not too hard (anxiety). Just right (Flow). Push yourself slightly outside your comfort zone. That is the sweet spot of growth."

**Script 3: Decision Fatigue**
*   **Audio:** "Why did Steve Jobs wear the same turtleneck every day? To avoid Decision Fatigue. You have a limited amount of decision energy. Don't waste it on clothes or breakfast. Automate the boring stuff so you can spend your energy on the big stuff. Simplify to amplify."

**Script 4: Sleep is not for the Weak**
*   **Audio:** "Hustle culture says 'I'll sleep when I'm dead.' Science says 'If you don't sleep, you'll be dead sooner.' Sleep is the number one performance enhancer. It cleans the brain, repairs the body, and consolidates memory. If you want to perform like an elite athlete, sleep like one."

**Script 5: Transcending Self**
*   **Audio:** "When the going gets tough, the ego quits. 'I'm tired.' 'I hurt.' But if you are doing it for something bigger than yourself—your family, your team, your god—you can push further. Purpose minimizes the sensation of pain. Find your 'Why' to hack your biology."
