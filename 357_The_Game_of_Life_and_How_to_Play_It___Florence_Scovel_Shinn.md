# The Game of Life and How to Play It by Florence Scovel Shinn

## 1. Long Detailed Summary for Blog

**Title:** Rules of the Game: *The Game of Life and How to Play It*

**Introduction**
Florence Scovel Shinn was an artist and metaphysical teacher in New York in the 1920s. Her style is conversational, funny, and deeply spiritual. She teaches that life is not a battle, but a game. However, you can't play the game if you don't know the rules (Spiritual Laws).

**Core Concepts**

1.  **The Law of Expectancy:**
    *   Whatever you expect with faith, you receive. "You are constantly reaping what you sow in your imagination."
    *   If you prepare for rain (carry an umbrella), you get rain. If you prepare for success, you get success.

2.  **The Square of Life:**
    *   True success has four sides: Health, Wealth, Love, and Perfect Self-Expression. If one is missing, the square is broken.

3.  **Words as Wands:**
    *   Your word is your wand. By speaking, you wave the wand. "I am always sick" creates sickness. "I am divinely protected" creates protection.
    *   Shinn provides many affirmations to reprogram the subconscious.

4.  **Non-Resistance:**
    *   "What you resist, persists." If you fight a situation, you give it power. If you bless it and stand aside, it loses its grip. "Stand still and see the salvation of the Lord."

**Conclusion**
Shinn’s book is a delight. It reminds us to lighten up, trust the Divine Design, and play the game with joy and faith.
