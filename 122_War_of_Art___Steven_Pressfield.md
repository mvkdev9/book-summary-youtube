# War of Art by Steven Pressfield

## 1. Long Detailed Summary for Blog

**Title:** Break Through the Blocks and Win Your Inner Creative Battles: *War of Art*

**Introduction**
Steven Pressfield identifies the #1 enemy of every creative person: **Resistance**. It is an invisible, universal, and malicious force that prevents us from doing our work. The book is a field manual for defeating Resistance and turning from an amateur into a professional.

**The Three Parts**
1.  **Resistance: Defining the Enemy:** Resistance is self-sabotage. It appears as procrastination, fear, self-doubt, and even physical illness. It is most powerful when you are close to a breakthrough.
2.  **Combating Resistance: Turning Pro:** The only way to beat Resistance is to become a professional. A pro shows up every day, works regardless of mood, and stays humble.
3.  **Beyond Resistance: The Higher Realm:** When we do our work, the universe helps us. Angels and Muses show up to guide the hand of the worker.

**Key Insights**
*   **The Amateur:** Works only when he feels like it. Seeks validation.
*   **The Professional:** Works because it’s his job. Accepts failure as part of the process.
*   **Rationalization:** Resistance’s right-hand man. It gives you "smart" reasons why you shouldn't work today.

**Conclusion**
The work is a war. You must show up at the desk every day and fight.
