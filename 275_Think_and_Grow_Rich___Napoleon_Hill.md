# Think and Grow Rich by Napoleon Hill

## 1. Long Detailed Summary for Blog

**Title:** The Landmark Bestseller Now Revised and Updated for the 21st Century: *Think and Grow Rich*

**Introduction**
Commissioned by Andrew Carnegie, Napoleon Hill spent 20 years studying 500 of the world's most successful men (Ford, Edison, Rockefeller). He identified 13 principles that lead to wealth. He argues that wealth begins with a state of mind.

**The 13 Principles (Selected)**
1.  **Desire:** The starting point of all achievement. You must have a "Burning Desire" for a specific goal.
2.  **Faith:** Visualization of, and belief in, the attainment of desire.
3.  **Auto-Suggestion:** Influencing the subconscious mind through repeated affirmations.
4.  **Specialized Knowledge:** General knowledge doesn't make you rich. You need a specific, high-value skill.
5.  **Imagination:** The workshop of the mind. Creating the plan to achieve the goal.
6.  **Persistence:** The power of will. Most people quit right before success.
7.  **The Master Mind:** Surrounding yourself with a group of people who support your vision and provide extra brainpower.

**Conclusion**
Whatever the mind can conceive and believe, it can achieve.
