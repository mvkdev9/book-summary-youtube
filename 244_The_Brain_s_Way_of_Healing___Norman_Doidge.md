# The Brain's Way of Healing by Norman Doidge

## 1. Long Detailed Summary for Blog

**Title:** Remarkable Discoveries and Recoveries from the Frontiers of Neuroplasticity: *The Brain's Way of Healing*

**Introduction**
In his second book, Norman Doidge focuses on "Bottom-Up" healing. He explores how simple, non-invasive therapies—like light, sound, and movement—can trigger the brain’s own healing mechanisms.

**Key Insights**
1.  **The Brain’s Energy:** The brain runs on energy. When it’s damaged (Parkinson’s, MS, Trauma), the energy becomes "noisy" or "chaotic."
2.  **Light Therapy:** Using low-level lasers (cold lasers) to provide energy directly to the mitochondria in brain cells. This can "wake up" dormant neurons.
3.  **The Tongue Device (PoNS):** A device that stimulates the tongue, which connects directly to the brainstem. This has helped people with MS and brain injuries regain balance.
4.  **Sound Therapy:** Using specific frequencies of music to treat autism and ADHD. The ear is a gateway to the nervous system.
5.  **Walking:** The story of a man who cured his Parkinson’s symptoms through conscious, high-intensity walking.

**Conclusion**
The brain has a natural ability to repair itself. By providing the right stimuli (light, sound, motion), we can unlock the "Pharmacy within."
