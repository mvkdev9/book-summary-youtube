# The Obesity Code by Jason Fung

## 1. Long Detailed Summary for Blog

**Title:** Unlocking the Secrets of Weight Loss: *The Obesity Code*

**Introduction**
Dr. Jason Fung, a nephrologist, argues that we have been fundamentally wrong about weight loss. Obesity is not a calorie problem; it is a **hormonal** problem. The primary driver of weight gain is **Insulin**.

**Key Insights**
1.  **The Insulin Theory of Obesity:** Every time you eat, insulin goes up. Insulin tells your body to store fat. If your insulin stays high all the time, you *cannot* lose weight, no matter how many calories you cut.
2.  **Insulin Resistance:** If you eat all day, your body becomes resistant to insulin. You need more and more to get the same result. This keeps your "Set Point" high.
3.  **The Set Point:** Your brain has a specific weight it wants you to be. If you cut calories, your brain just slows down your metabolism to match. You have to lower your insulin to lower your set point.
4.  **Cortisol:** Stress raises cortisol, which raises insulin. Stress makes you fat.
5.  **The Solution:** Intermittent Fasting. Fasting is the only way to lower insulin levels long enough for the body to burn its own fat and reset the set point.

**Conclusion**
Weight loss is about *When* you eat, not just *What* you eat.
